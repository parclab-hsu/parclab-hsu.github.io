Research

Research Areas

Our research integrates AI intelligence, deterministic communication, control architecture, and robotic deployment into one continuous engineering pipeline.

Physical AI Systems

Embodied intelligence, perception-planning-control integration, and real-world autonomy for robotic systems.

Real-Time Control

Deterministic control loops, motion synchronization, and industrial-grade control architecture for safety-critical systems.

EtherCAT Network

EtherCAT master/slave integration, high-speed communication, distributed I/O, and synchronized fieldbus control.

Mobility Platforms

Autonomous mobility, smart assistive mobility, and intelligent vehicle platform control for practical environments.

Robotics

Manipulators, surgical robotics, service robots, and multi-axis motion systems with high precision and safety.

Digital Twin & HILS

Simulation-to-reality verification, hardware-in-the-loop integration, and digital twin validation workflows.
